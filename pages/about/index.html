With a focus on innovation and precision, our expert team ensures the delivery of durable, aesthetically pleasing, and energy-efficient solutions.


The term “facade” is derived from the French word “façade”, which translates to “face”. In architectural terms, a facade is not just the front elevation of a building but any side that faces a public space or an external environment. A facade is often composed of several elements:

  • Wall surfaces:These can be composed of brick, stone, glass, metal, or a combination of materials.
  • Openings:Windows, doors, and other penetrations that provide light, access, or ventilation
  • Ornaments:Decorative elements such as cornices, mouldings, and sculptures.
  • Functional features: Aspects like louvers, sunshades, or shutters that have specific utilities.
